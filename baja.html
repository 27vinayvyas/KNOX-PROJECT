ABOUT THE EVENTS



Largest collegiate design competition in the world held at national & international levels in various countries Requires small teams of students to design, fabricate, test, race & market a single seat ATV designed for recreational purpose within a set of rules. Organized annually since 2007 by SAE India at NATRIP, Pithampur (Near Indore, M.P.) Baja SAE India 2016 saw participation of 350+ teams from across India including IITs, NITs & Major Private Engineering colleges A footfall of 6,000+ students, faculty & corporate delegates was recorded in a 4 day event held in January Split into two events, Virtual Baja (held in July) & Final Event (held in February)




VIRTUALS
Elimination round with 400+ teams judged on their design & CAE skills held in Chandigarh at Chitkara University (2017)


MAIN EVENT
Held near Indore & Ropar. Events Divided into two categories i.e. Static & Dynamic. 152 teams are shortlisted from Virtuals to participate

STATIC EVENT
Comprises of Design Evaluation, CAE Evaluation, Build Quality Test, Ecological Idea presentation, Sales Presentation & A Technical Inspection of the vehicle (Days 1 & 2)

DYNAMIC EVENT
Comprises of various tests including Braking, Acceleration, Hill Climb, Suspension, Maneuverability & Traction & a 4-hour long Endurance Race (Days 3 & 4)
